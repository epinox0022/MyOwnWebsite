grumpig

Grumpig is a Psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Generation III of the Pokémon series. Grumpig resembles a pig-like creature with a prominent snout and a curled tail.

 

ditto

Ditto is a Normal-type Pokémon in the Pokémon series. Ditto's transformation ability allows it to copy the appearance, moves, and even the stats of the Pokémon it's transforming into.

 

arbok

Arbok is a Poison-type Pokémon in the Pokémon series.Arbok is known for its powerful Poison-type attacks and its ability to intimidate opponents with its menacing appearance.
